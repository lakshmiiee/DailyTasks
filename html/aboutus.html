ABOUT US - Times of India

The Brand

The Times of India Group


India's "Largest Media Conglomerate", The Times of India Group has been publishing newspapers and magazines since 1838. The Group's print properties include The Times of India, The Economic Times, Navbharat Times, Sandhya Times, Mumbai Mirror, Bangalore Mirror, and Maharashtra Times. The Group has also diversified into internet (which includes the portal www.indiatimes.com), radio (Radio Mirchi), television (Times Now, Zoom) and other related media activities like Times Music. World Wide Media, which owns Femina and Filmfare among other magazines, is a joint venture between The Times Group and BBC.

Who are we?

Times Syndication Service


The syndication division of The Times of India Group, Times Syndication Service (TSS), is in the business of distributing high-quality content for use in print and online mediums. Our content offering (text, images, cartoons/illustrations, audio/video and microfilm) is drawn primarily from Times Group publications. Our clients comprise print publications, internet sites, content aggregators, libraries and research organizations. We also provide service to organizations/corporates that have a need for content to serve internal knowledge needs. In our endeavour to showcase our exclusive content and make them available for syndication for our global clients, we bring to you www.timescontent.com
