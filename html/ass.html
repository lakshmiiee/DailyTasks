VIVO SMARTPHONES

In 2023, Vivo introduced several 5G models, including the vivo T4x 5G, vivo T3 Pro 5G, vivo T4 5G, and vivo T3 5G. The vivo T4x 5G, for example, is priced starting from ₹13,999, while the vivo T3 Pro 5G is available from ₹24,999. These models offer features like fast charging, 5G connectivity, and varying RAM and storage options.
